Know Us !

  • Phoenix
The Technical Club of Netaji Subhash Engineering College, the people who make this Avenir possible !

Phoenix is the official tech-club of Netaji Subhash Engineering College. It was established in 2006 under the able leadership of a few seniors of the college (seven to be precise). Starting from just seven, in eight years, Phoenix has grown to be the tech - hub of NSEC with a membership of over 300 students from various streams, and a full hierarchy of management.

Students indulged in ‘Phoenix-ing’ tend to develop various skills which boost their personality and shape up their professional career. They develop a sense of camaraderie, team spirit while working together in organising various events.
